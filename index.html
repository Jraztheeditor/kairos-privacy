Kairos

Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 2026  ·  iOS App

The short version: Kairos collects no personal data, sends nothing to any server, and requires no account. Everything you enter stays on your device — always.

1. Overview

Kairos ("we," "our," or "the app") is a scheduling, calendar, and habit-tracking application for iOS. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app accesses, how it is used, and your rights with respect to that information.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Kairos is designed so that your personal data never leaves your device.

2. Data We Collect

Kairos stores only the information you explicitly provide while using the app. All data is saved locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework and iOS UserDefaults. This includes:

  • Your name (entered during onboarding, stored in UserDefaults)
  • Onboarding completion status (stored in UserDefaults)
  • Schedules, events, and calendar entries you create
  • Habit definitions and completion records you log
  • Notification preferences and reminder settings you configure
Stored on-device only No cloud sync No server transmission No account required

We do not collect, access, or store:

  • Location data
  • Contacts, photos, or media library content
  • Device identifiers, IP addresses, or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports sent to our servers
  • Any biometric or health data

3. How We Use Your Data

All data stored by Kairos is used solely to provide the core functionality of the app — displaying your schedule, tracking your habits, and sending local notifications you have configured.

Local Notifications: Kairos may request permission to send you notifications via Apple's UNUserNotificationCenter. These notifications are scheduled and delivered entirely on your device. No notification content or delivery status is transmitted to any external server.

Your data is never used for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app on your device.

4. Third-Party Services

Kairos does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, crash-reporting, or data-collection services. There are no third-party SDKs embedded in the app that collect or transmit data.

The only external service involved in delivering Kairos is the Apple App Store. Apple's own privacy practices govern the distribution process. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for details.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

All data created in Kairos persists on your device for as long as the app is installed. You can delete individual entries at any time within the app, or use Settings → Data → Reset App Data to erase everything at once.

To permanently remove all data, simply uninstall the app. Because we hold no copies of your data on any server, uninstalling the app completely and irrevocably removes all associated data.

6. Children's Privacy

Kairos does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13, or under the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction. Because the app collects no personal data and requires no account, it presents no special privacy risk to younger users.

7. Data Security

Because all data remains on your device, it is protected by the security features built into iOS, including device passcode encryption and the iOS app sandbox. We encourage you to use a strong passcode and keep your operating system up to date. Kairos requires iOS 17 or later.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of Kairos after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
